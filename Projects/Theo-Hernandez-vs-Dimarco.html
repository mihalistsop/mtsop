comparison

The cards displaying the ratio of successful/unsuccessful long passes and forward zone passes attempted by both players provides a detailed breakdown of their passing accuracy in different areas of the field.

 By examining the values in the cards, you can assess the success rates of each player in executing long passes and forward zone passes.

This visualization allows you to compare the players' passing abilities, identify their strengths and weaknesses in different zones, and gain insights into their decision-making when distributing the ball. 

The table offers a comprehensive view of Theo Hernández and Federico Dimarco's passing accuracy, allowing for a thorough analysis of their performance and its impact on the game.

The bubble chart comparing the number of tackles, fouls committed, and yellow cards received by each player provides valuable insights into their defensive performance and disciplinary records. 

The size of each bubble represents the number of the respective metric, allowing for a quick assessment of players' disciplinary tendencies or aggressive playing styles. 

By examining the positions and sizes of the bubbles, it becomes easy to compare players and identify those with strong defensive skills :muscle: and good discipline, as well as areas for improvement. 

Overall, this visualization offers a comprehensive overview of players' defensive contributions, discipline, and the balance between effective tackling and committing fouls.
