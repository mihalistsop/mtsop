Unleashing the Brilliance of Onana and Ederson using Microsoft Power BI

Performance Overview

  ​Generally, both Ederson and Onana exhibit exceptional goalkeeping prowess, demonstrating their capacity to execute vital saves and bolster their team's defensive performance. Although each goalkeeper possesses unique strengths and areas that could be refined, their contributions are indispensable in safeguarding their team's goal and substantially influencing match results. Their remarkable displays significantly shape the dynamics of their respective teams, underscoring the crucial role a dependable goalkeeper plays in upholding a formidable defensive line.

Shot-stopping Analysis

  The examination of the "saves" metric, which evaluates the shot-stopping abilities of Onana and Ederson, highlights their impressive skills in saving shots across various types. Both goalkeepers excel in stopping shots within the penalty box, effectively handling long-range strikes, and executing diving saves. These abilities greatly enhance their teams' defensive capabilities, demonstrating their competence in thwarting a wide array of shots and consistently delivering strong performances as goalkeepers.

Distribution Accuracy

  The assessment of goalkeepers' distribution skills, measured by the "totalLongBalls" and "accurateLongBalls" metrics, offers valuable insights into their capacity to spark attacking plays and create goal-scoring opportunities for their respective teams. Ederson exhibits a superior accuracy in long ball distribution compared to Onana, yet both goalkeepers play vital roles in their teams' offensive strategies. Their precise delivery of long balls enables them to bypass opponents' midfield lines and directly supply their teammates with scoring chances, underscoring their pivotal role in initiating attacking movements. These distribution skills, in conjunction with attributes like vision and decision-making, form an integral part of their teams' overall gameplay and significantly contribute to their effectiveness as goalkeepers.

Goals Conceded Analysis

  Both goalkeepers exhibit a remarkable ability to effectively thwart goals, albeit with minor fluctuations in performance from season to season. Their contributions play a crucial role in bolstering their teams' defensive endeavors, emphasizing the significance of a solid defensive line and the minimization of the opposition's scoring opportunities.

Penalty Performance 

  Upon analyzing the penaltyFaced and penaltySave metrics, it becomes apparent that only Ederson has encountered penalty situations. However, neither Ederson nor Onana have registered any penalty saves based on the data available. Although the absence of penalty saves may indicate that both goalkeepers haven't been successful in stopping penalties during the examined period, it is crucial to take into account that penalty saves can be relatively infrequent and contingent on several factors. Elements such as the penalty taker's skill, the goalkeeper's positioning, anticipation, and even a bit of luck can influence the outcome of penalty situations. As such, the lack of recorded penalty saves should be interpreted with consideration for these variables.
