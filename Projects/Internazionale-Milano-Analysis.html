Performance Analysis for Internazionale Milano

General Information about the analysis

In this analysis, we delve into the performance of Inter in the past season to gain insights into their strengths, weaknesses
and overall team dynamics.

By examining various metrics and statistics, I aim to provide a comprehensive assessment of the team's performance using mostly Tableau and Microsoft Excel.

To conduct this analysis, we gathered a vast array of data from Inter's matches throughout the season, with the help of the infamous and accurate Sport Data Collecting company, Opta. This data includes Match results, Player statistics and Team formations. 

The dataset was carefully curated and organized to ensure accuracy and relevance. I utilized various sources, such as official match reports, player performance databases, and sports analytics websites, to compile a comprehensive and reliable dataset for our analysis.

In this analysis, we will be able to determine the side of the field from which Inter attacked the most during the matches of the tournament.

We focused on key performance metrics to evaluate Inter's performance in both Left and Right side of the pitch. 

The table will provide a clear overview of the attacking sides and their respective frequencies, enabling an understanding of Inter's preferred direction of attack throughout the tournament.

In this analysis, we will be able to rank INTER's players based on their performance in attempting accurate long passes during the matches of the tournament.

The treemap visualization will visually represent the rankings, highlighting the players who excelled in distributing the ball effectively over longer distances.  

In this analysis, we focus on Inter player's ability to regain possession of the ball successfully in the middle third of the field. By examining this specific aspect of their gameplay, we aim to understand their effectiveness in disrupting opponent's attacks and initiating their own offensive moves.

TreeMap of Successful Ball Regains

To present the findings in an insightful and visually appealing manner, we created a treemap visualization. The treemap provides a hierarchical representation of the successful ball regains, with the size of each rectangle corresponding to the frequency or quantity of regains. The treemap allows for a quick and intuitive understanding of the distribution and importance of successful regains of the middle third based on the players' position in the match.

We will be able to present the rankings in a treemap, highlighting the players who have accurate long balls greater than zero and their sum of possessions won in the middle third.

 The treemap visualization offers a clear and concise representation of the data, providing valuable insights into the areas of the middle third where Manchester City displayed exceptional performance. Understanding these patterns can inform future tactical decisions and further enhance the team's overall gameplay strategy.
