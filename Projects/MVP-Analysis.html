Winning Team & Goals Scored

  The table showcases the team names and their corresponding goal counts, providing insights into the ultimate outcome of the match. By analyzing the data, we can determine which team emerged victorious or suffered a loss. The table allows for easy comparison between teams and their goal-scoring abilities, providing a clear representation of the match results.

Passing Efficiency

  The gauge chart facilitates a quick assessment of the pass completion rate, with the needle indicating the specific percentage. This visual representation simplifies the identification of players with a higher pass completion rate, showcasing their proficiency in maintaining possession and executing accurate passes.

Offensive Contributions

  The clustered bar chart facilitates a straightforward comparison of the three metrics - total scoring attempts, goals scored, and goal assists. By presenting these metrics side by side, we can obtain a comprehensive understanding of a team or player's offensive contributions. Through analysis of the chart, we can identify teams or players with a high number of total scoring attempts, noteworthy goal-scoring records, and substantial contributions in terms of goal assists.

Defensive Prowess

  Through the analysis of the chart, we can make comparisons between the defensive performances of different players and identify those who consistently display robust defensive skills across both metrics. This enables us to pinpoint players who excel in both won tackles and aerial duels won, highlighting their versatility and effectiveness in various defensive situations.
