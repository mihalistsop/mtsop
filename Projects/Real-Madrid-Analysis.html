Performance Analysis for Real Madrid

General Information about the analysis

This analysis focuses on Real Madrid's performance in the tournament. It aims to provide insights into the team's overall performance, including their goal-scoring ability, defensive strength,
effectiveness in creating and converting big chances.

The analysis considers key metrics such as: Total goals scored, Goals conceded, Total big chances created, Total big chances missed, Total accurate passes, Total accurate Att Assists, Total touches in Opposite's penalty area, Total accurate Long passes.

By examining these metrics, we can assess Real Madrid's attacking prowess, defensive stability, and their ability to capitalize on scoring opportunities. The analysis helps identify the team's strengths and areas for improvement, providing valuable insights for team management and strategic decision-making. 

Additionally, visual representations such as bar charts, scatter plots, and tables are utilized to present the data effectively, enabling a comprehensive understanding of Real Madrid's performance throughout the tournament. The analysis serves as a valuable resource for evaluating the team's overall performance and identifying key areas to focus on for future success.

Passing and Playmaking

based on each player

The Passing and Playmaking scatter plot visualizes the relationship between accurate passes (x-axis) and total attacking assists (y-axis). 

Each data point represents a player, and the plot provides insights into the connection between passing accuracy and playmaking contributions. It allows for identifying players with high passing accuracy who also make significant contributions in creating scoring opportunities for their team.

The scatter plot helps assess the effectiveness of players in terms of passing and playmaking skills, highlighting individuals who excel in both areas.

The chart showcases the distribution of player touches in the opponent's penalty box among players who have scored at least one goal. 

Each circle represents a player's share of touches within the penalty box, highlighting their scoring contributions. The size of each circle reflects the relative proportion of touches for each player. 

This chart provides insights into both the scoring prowess and efficiency of individual players. It presents how players who have found the back of the net contribute to attacking plays and their effectiveness in generating goal-scoring opportunities within the opponent's penalty box. 

Scoring Prowess: The chart highlights players who have scored at least one goal.
